Source Verification Workflow

Five-step research process used to build and validate every record in the database.

1

Directory Discovery

Find doctors from concierge medicine networks and public directories including MDVIP, SignatureMD, and PartnerMD.

2

Independent Practice Search

Research independent concierge and membership-based practices state by state and city by city.

3

Practice Website Validation

Confirm that each practice offers concierge, membership-based, or direct primary care-style services.

4

Contact Data Extraction

Collect email, phone, address, city, state, and source URL from publicly available pages only.

5

Cleaning & Export

Remove duplicates, flag missing fields, sort by state and city, and export to Excel or CSV.

Recommended Phased Delivery

A two-phase approach ensures quality before scaling to all 50 states.

Phase 1

Sample State Pilot

Research and verify one selected state first to confirm data quality, format, and sourcing method before scaling.

  • One target state
  • Verified doctor/practice records
  • Source URLs included
  • Missing field flags applied
  • Duplicate check completed
  • Excel/CSV sample delivered
Phase 2

Full 50-State Database

After approval of the sample, expand the same verified research process across all 50 US states.

  • All 50 states
  • Network and independent practices
  • Manual + automated research support
  • Quality control review
  • Final cleaned Excel/CSV file
  • Ongoing update option available
